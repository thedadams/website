      <p class="flex-1">
        <a href="/minimal.html" title="Minimal Watchface" class="text-blue-400 hover:underline font-bold text-xl">Minimal</a> is a simple watchface. It displays the time, date, and day of the week. You can choose whether the number in the middle represents the hour or the minutes; the "hand" will represent the other. Minimal also has a few other customizable options such as hourly vibrations, bluetooth connection vibrations, battery charging indicator, and battery meter in the hand.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-8">
      <a href="/contrastdots.html" title="Contrast Dots Watchface">
        <img alt="Contrast Dots Screenshot" class="mx-auto w-48 md:w-64" src="/assets/images/pebble-screenshot_2014-07-13_11-16-44.png"/>
      </a>
      <p class="flex-1">
        <a href="/contrastdots.html" title="Contrast Dots Watchface" class="text-blue-400 hover:underline font-bold text-xl">Contrast Dots</a> displays the time in a unique way using a grid of dots. There are two sections of dots which have contrasting design: one for the hour and the other for the minutes. It also has a battery meter built into the hour section.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-8">
      <a href="/verticaldials.html" title="Vertical Dials Watchface">
        <img alt="Vertical Dials Screenshot" class="mx-auto w-48 md:w-58" src="/assets/images/pebble-screenshot_2014-07-18_20-50-49.png"/>
      </a>
      <p class="flex-1">
        <a href="/verticaldials.html" title="Vertical Dials Watchface" class="text-blue-400 hover:underline font-bold text-xl">Vertical Dials</a> uses two dials to give time. The triangular indicators point to the current hour and minutes. The center section holds the day of the week, date, and battery meter.
      </p>
