# Watchfaces for Pebble

- [![Minimal Screenshot](/assets/images/pebble-screenshot_2014-06-14_16-10-09.png)](/minimal)

  [**Minimal**](/minimal) is a simple watchface. It displays the time, date, and day of the week. You can choose whether the number in the middle represents the hour or the minutes; the "hand" will represent the other. Minimal also has a few other customizable options such as hourly vibrations, bluetooth connection vibrations, battery charging indicator, and battery meter in the hand.
- [![Contrast Dots Screenshot](/assets/images/pebble-screenshot_2014-07-13_11-16-44.png)](/contrastdots)

  [**Contrast Dots**](/contrastdots) displays the time in a unique way using a grid of dots. There are two sections of dots which have contrasting design: one for the hour and the other for the minutes. It also has a battery meter built into the hour section.
- [![Vertical Dials Screenshot](/assets/images/pebble-screenshot_2014-07-18_20-50-49.png)](/verticaldials)

  [**Vertical Dials**](/verticaldials) uses two dials to give time. The triangular indicators point to the current hour and minutes. The center section holds the day of the week, date, and battery meter.
